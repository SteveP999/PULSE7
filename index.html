About PULSE7

PULSE7 is a seven-member K-Pop group signed to Hello Texas Records, built on the belief that great music speaks every language. Their sound fuses tight vocal harmonies, cinematic production, and the kind of choreographic energy that turns a song into an experience.

From their debut singles Signal Locked and We Don't Miss to their latest release Two Nights, PULSE7 has been building toward something bigger — a full-length debut album that promises to be one of the most ambitious releases in the label's history.

Endless Echo is coming. Stay tuned.
